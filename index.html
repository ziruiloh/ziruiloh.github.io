Daily Digest

Your AI-Powered Daily News Summarizer and Translator

Daily Digest AI is an intelligent news platform that uses Google's Gemini AI to deliver personalized news summaries across seven categories—from world events to technology. The app features real-time translation capabilities where users can click any word to instantly translate it into seven languages, making global news accessible to everyone. Built with React and featuring a sleek, responsive design, it's perfect for busy professionals who want concise, AI-curated news updates without information overload.

Career Catalyst

Career Preparation Plan In One Click

Get from "I have these skills" to "I'm ready for this job" in 3 simple steps. Career Catalyst is an AI-powered career development tool that transforms your skills into a winning job strategy in minutes. Simply enter your skills (like "React, Node.js, AWS") and instantly get personalized job recommendations with detailed role descriptions and skill gap analysis. The app then generates custom study plans with week-by-week learning roadmaps tailored to your experience level and available time, complete with interactive to-do lists you can check off as you progress.
